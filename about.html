About Vipho

Real-time creator hiring platform • Built under Wencuts

Vipho is a next-generation creator hiring and media-tech platform designed to connect customers with photographers, videographers, and digital creators in real time.

Vipho is a product brand developed and operated under Wencuts, a creative design and digital solutions company.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower the creator economy by making it simple, fast, and secure for customers to discover and hire verified creators, while enabling creators to earn more through transparent and efficient digital workflows.

What We Are Building

  • Real-time discovery and booking of creators
  • Instant and secure payments
  • Verified profiles, ratings, and portfolios
  • End-to-end workflows for media creation and delivery

Why We Collect Contributions

Contributions collected through Vipho are used to support:

  • Platform and mobile app development
  • Technology infrastructure and scaling
  • Creator onboarding and community growth
  • Marketing, outreach, and user acquisition

These contributions do not automatically provide ownership, equity, or guaranteed financial returns unless explicitly stated in a separate signed agreement.
